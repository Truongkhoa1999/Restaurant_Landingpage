ABOUT US

With 20 years of experience in the culinary industry, we ourselves have embraced cross- cultures to ensure a diverse food carpet. We offer eaters to enjoy different type of food via various theme table types. You can also customize the theme according to your wishes. Make sure your selection is the most comfortable.

Beverages

Drinks included during the meal will be refilled. For a clear view of the basic drinks in the package, please pay attention to the table-type service. There will be an additional charge for private drinks buffet. Wine is the ultimate drink for every table type

Traditional food

Traditional foods to many cultures will depend on seasonal ingredients. However, we will guarantee their scarcity, as long as you follow the restaurant's navigational options. And if there are any changes to your mind or creativity in traditional dishes please notify us soon. We want to make sure your creativity and choices reflect the future meal well

Dessert

Dessert is available on your table only when you decide to make an offer. That is, desserts can include fruit-based dishes. You should only order them 10 minutes before the main meal to ensure their freshness. However, choosing to enjoy desserts parallel with a main meal is not bad.

Vegetarian meals

Diet is a method for good health. The way of using vegetarian dishes is also different from region to region. Please advise us about your wished ingredients for vegetarian food. We'll listen and advise on the essential ingredients in your meals to ensure nutrition and deliciousness.

Cakes

Besides the cakes in the menu, you will feel free to choose a cake according to your style and preferences. Talk to the customer service department and reflect on the type of cake you want. We will take note.

Services

Transportation services are completely free within 20km, entertainment services attached to meals will not be surcharged during fixed hours. If you want to specialize shows according to your private meal time, fee is required.
